Pichu, Pikachu, Raichu

Description

Pichu is small and playful but struggles to control its electricity, often shocking itself by accident. As it evolves into Pikachu, it learns to store and release electricity more efficiently, using its tail to communicate with others. When it reaches its final form, Raichu, its power surges dramatically, allowing it to unleash powerful electric attacks while using its tail to ground excess energy.
